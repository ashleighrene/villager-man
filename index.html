Villager Man

How to Play Villager Man

Try to keep as many villagers on your island as you can!

The computer will pick a random word and you get to try and guess it.

Type in a letter that you think is in the word.

If the selected letter is in the word, it will be revealed in the word box. If it is not in the hidden word, it will go into the trash heap and a villager will leave the island.

If all villagers leave, then the game is over and you lose. If you guess the word and at least one villager is still on your island, you win!
